Greatest People of India:

Mahatma Gandhi

Leader of the Indian independence movement against British rule, known for his philosophy of non-violence.

Dr. B.R. Ambedkar

Social reformer and the principal architect of the Indian Constitution, he fought for the rights of the marginalized.

Mother Teresa

Renowned humanitarian and missionary known for her work with the poor and sick in Kolkata.

Rabindranath Tagore

Poet, writer, and philosopher, he was the first non-European to win the Nobel Prize in Literature in 1913.

Swami Vivekananda

Philosopher and spiritual leader who introduced Indian philosophies of Vedanta and Yoga to the Western world.

Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel

Poet, writer, and philosopher, he was the first non-European to win the Nobel Prize in Literature in 1913.
